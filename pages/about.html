About Us

Impact Labs was founded in a Brown University dorm room by two friends, Aaron and Adi, who were frustrated that talented computer science students wanted to use their skills to make meaningful social impact, yet they often ended up flocking to Silicon Valley giants post-graduation.

The mission of Impact Labs is to empower and inspire talented technologists to use their skills for the betterment of society and the planet. Since our inception, we have hosted hundreds of attendees across our in-person and virtual Impact Summits, connected 200+ fellows as part of our four Impact Fellowships, and built up a network of ~10,000 technologists, partners, and creators.

Our Core Values

Intentionality

We value intentionality and thoughtfulness in everything we do. Social impact is multifaceted and complex, and we strive to understand its nuances, respect its different forms, and recognize the structural challenges in place that make progress nonlinear.

Dreaming Big

We love to dream beyond conventional norms. Our hope is to open the doors of possibility for young change-makers who are eager to leverage their skillsets in meaningful careers. Building platforms for high-risk refugee populations. Developing software solutions to improve voter turnout. Engineering new solar panel distribution systems. The possibilities are endless!
